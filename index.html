Community Security

Welcome to the unofficial site for Another Axiom's handpicked group of developers, Community Security!


Our Mission

Supporting Another Axiom's games by identifying and addressing security vulnerabilities, promoting best practices, and working collaboratively to help maintain the security and reliability of their games.


Important things to note:
  • While this website isn't official, the Community Security group is 100% official and recognized by Another Axiom.
  • Being a member of Community Security does not mean we are contracted or employed by Another Axiom.
  • We are not an alternative way to report misbehaving users or exploits. If you have such information, please reach out to modmail instead.
